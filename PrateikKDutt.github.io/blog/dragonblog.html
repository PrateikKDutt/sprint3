A Drake

The Four Legs and Two winged dragon

The Drake is a the most common type of creature we think of when we think of dragons.They are the most dangers type of dragon to run across.These proud beasts have very good eyesight and half their time in the air and half running on ground like a horse.

These creatures are not aggassive on their own but will attack if they see someone they like, this is unfortunately, anything shinny like gold or silver

These tihngs include but are not limited to

  • Golden rings
  • golden neckless
  • golden hair
  • bright yellow paint
  • silver plates
  • Any kind of precious jewel

However if you find yourself face-to-face with a drake we recommend you avoid doing the following 4 thing

  1. Running at it with a sword
  2. Trying to pet it
  3. Trying to steal any gold you see around it
  4. yelling at it

Dispite the risks, riding drakes have becoming a popular pastime with several song being written about this activity.However we do caution you dear reader not to try this as while the few success are sung about, the likelyhood of death is usually 4 out of 6 times.
